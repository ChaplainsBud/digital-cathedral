Software Lifecyle: Dockers/Kubernetes

This is a blueprint for all digital religious buildings. The goal here is to scale architecture down to the individual monk cell all the way up to an epic cathedral. Actually, the hermetic desert monk will be granted a place as well -- solitude will be communal for all, but in a different way.

We are almost all online, so this will help those with a religious bent find their way home, rather than get lost in the distraction of materialism, be it substance abuse, sexual addition, anxiety-driven learning, fear driven news reading, as well as taking an aggressive stance towards online shopping, stealing, vandalism through building viruses, and so on. This is an oasis for those who want to come back home, as well as those who have nevver been home in the first place -- old and new religionists.

You also won't find mocha/chai/jest testing. Nor will you see Heroku deployment, or Jenkins CI. Those are all in my mind though, and will eventually show up. Maybe even jQuery will come up, and most likely reactstrap combining Bootstrap 4 with React. Redux.
